# ABOUT MANUKORA
We're a New Zealand honey brand selling DTC on Shopify and on Amazon — mostly in the US. We have a monthly S&OP meeting where the team reviews sales, stock levels, and reorder decisions. Right now it's mostly manual — someone pulls data from Shopify and our inventory system (Cin7), drops it into a spreadsheet, and presents to the team. It takes hours and the insight isn't always there.

## PART 1
Build it (mandatory): Using the mock data, build an AI agent or automation that generates a weekly S&OP briefing that a non-technical exec can read in 5 minutes and act on.

At minimum, the output should:
- Surface what sold well vs. poorly over the past month, with trend across prior months
- Flag SKUs where stock cover is at risk based on current sell-through rate
- Make a reorder recommendation for at least 3 SKUs, with reasoning

The LLM must do genuine reasoning — not just reformat the data. Show us the prompt(s) you used. Use whatever tools you want.

## PART 2
Architect the next thing (no build required): Describe how you'd build a "Morning Intelligence Brief" — a daily automated message sent to our CEO at 6am NZ that surfaces the 3 most important things he needs to know about the business that day. Pull from Shopify + at least one other source. 500-800 words, concise and specific. Cover what you'd build, tools/APIs, rough cost, failure modes.
